Visual Hierarchy

Domino's Pizza
https://www.dominos.com/

Visual hierarchy is the order in which human eye perceives what it sees.

Domino’s pizza website follows the principle of visual hierarchy with the background picture which the user sees first, then the price with the size and the color, than the button “order now” in red and the text around.

Proximity

Family Search
https://www.familysearch.org/en/

Proximity is designers move elements closer together or farther apart. Proximity is use to create connections or deploy them.

Family search website follows the principle of proximity by highlighting the message that people can buy ticket to participate at RootsTech and give brief explication

White Space and Clean Design

Redbuble
https://www.redbubble.com/fr

White space is the blank area around, inside, or between the elements of a design.

Redbuble website follows the principle of white space with the blank area in the header and under the banner picture.
